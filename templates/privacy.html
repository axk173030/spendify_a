Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information — your name and email address, provided at registration.
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter manually.
  • Usage data — basic logs such as login timestamps and page visits, used to maintain service reliability.

We do not collect payment information, location data, or any data from your device beyond what is listed above.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to provide and improve the Spendly service:

  • To display your expenses, summaries, and category breakdowns.
  • To authenticate your account and keep it secure.
  • To diagnose technical issues and improve app performance.

We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not sell or rent it to any third party.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. We apply industry-standard measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and access controls to protect your information. While we take reasonable precautions, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly uses a small number of third-party services to operate:

  • Google Fonts — to load typefaces. Google may log font requests per their own privacy policy.

No analytics platforms, ad networks, or social tracking scripts are embedded in Spendly.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you. You may also withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account. We will fulfil any such requests within a reasonable timeframe.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the support contact listed on our website. We aim to respond within 5 business days.
